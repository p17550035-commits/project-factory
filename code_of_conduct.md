# Code of Conduct

This document outlines the expected behavior for all contributors and participants in the Project Factory ecosystem.  
The goal is to maintain a respectful, productive, and safe environment.

---

# Our Standards

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and considerate  
- Use inclusive language  
- Accept constructive feedback  
- Focus on collaboration  
- Avoid disruptive behavior  
- Maintain professionalism  

---

# Unacceptable Behavior

The following actions are not permitted:

- Harassment or discrimination  
- Personal attacks  
- Trolling or inflammatory comments  
- Sharing private information  
- Threats or intimidation  
- Malicious or harmful actions  

---

# Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience a violation:

- Contact the project owner privately  
- Provide details and context  
- Include screenshots or logs if possible  
- Do not escalate publicly  

All reports will be reviewed confidentially.

---

# Enforcement

Violations may result in:

- Warnings  
- Temporary restrictions  
- Removal from the project  
- Permanent bans in severe cases  

Enforcement decisions are final.

---

# Notes

This Code of Conduct may evolve as the project grows.  
Updates will be documented in the changelog.
